What's this cause about?

You have a new neighbor! Someone just moved off the streets into your nearby area and it’s time to party! Make a difference in their lives by throwing them a Welcome Home Party! To get started, simply select “Accept Cause” and it will send the invitation to the rest of your group.

Before the party, your group is responsible for putting together a Welcome Home Kit to make this apartment a HOME! Please be sure your group has a full box ready to go, and it’s Party Time!
